Precision

Our focus on — accuracy and confidence

EfficientNet-B3 trained on 25,331 ISIC 2019 dermoscopy images achieves 85% weighted F1. Every prediction surfaces per-class confidence scores so you can see exactly where the model is certain — and where it isn't.

Balanced accuracy of 77.8% with test-time augmentation means real diagnostic signal even on rare lesion types that make up under 1% of the dataset.

Explainability

Our focus on — transparent AI decisions

Grad-CAM heatmaps highlight the exact regions of your image that drove the model's prediction — not just a label, but a visual map of the evidence the network attended to.

This lets clinicians and researchers validate the model's reasoning, catch shortcuts, and build trust in AI-assisted screening over time.
